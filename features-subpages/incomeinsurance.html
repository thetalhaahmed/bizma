What is Business Income Insurance?

Business Income coverage helps replace income lost when a business is unable to operate due to a covered property loss such as damage from a fire, theft or wind.

Best of all, business income coverage is already included in a Business Owner's Policy from The Hartford. And you can easily increase coverage to meet specific risks to your business income by choosing from a variety of optional business income coverages that can be added as endorsements to your existing Business Owner's Policy.

Who needs Business Income Coverage?

If your business relies on a physical location, equipment or other property to generate income, consider increasing your business income coverage with endorsements to your existing Business Owner's Policy.
